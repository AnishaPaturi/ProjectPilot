Project Title

ProjectPilot AI

AI Agent for IEEE Research Paper Recommendation and Major Project Planning
Problem Statement

Final-year students spend days or weeks searching for a suitable project paper.

Current problems:

Students don't know which domain to choose.
Many select conference papers by mistake.
Papers may be too difficult to implement.
Students accidentally pick topics already chosen by classmates.
Faculty often reject papers because they are surveys or old publications.
Students don't know whether a paper has scope for innovation.

ProjectPilot AI solves this by automatically recommending suitable IEEE journal papers based on student interests and constraints.
